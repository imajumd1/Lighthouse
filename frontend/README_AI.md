# Lighthouse - AI Market Intelligence Platform

Lighthouse is a curated, data-rich feed of the top ten AI market trends for consulting executives.

## Features

- **Top Ten Trends Feed:** A curated list of the most impactful AI trends.
- **Detailed Analysis:** In-depth breakdown of why it's a trend, how consultancies can leverage it, and affected verticals.
- **Search & Filter:** Find trends by keyword or vertical.
- **Bookmarks:** Save trends to your personal library.
- **Admin Dashboard:** Manage trends and fetch metadata from URLs.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign In:** Use the credentials above to sign in.
2. **Browse Trends:** Explore the top ten trends on the home page.
3. **Filter:** Use the search bar or vertical chips to filter the feed.
4. **View Details:** Click on a trend to see the full analysis.
5. **Bookmark:** Save interesting trends to your "Saved" list.
6. **Admin:** Log in as the admin to access the dashboard and add new trends.
